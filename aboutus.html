ABOUT US
MISSION:

The goal is to provide information regarding services to help improve the City of Austin's homelessness populations. We hope to continue to help the lives of homeless people who suffer in Austin everyday through this website.

DIRECTION:

HomeAID is created by a high school student with a determined mindset to help others in need, specifically those dealing with homelessness. On a daily basis, HomeAID seeks to improve the visual efficiency of our system and update services. This enables good communication between services and the homeless.

CONTACT INFO:

To add a service, please fill out the form on our Add A Service Page. If you wish to contact us regarding something else, email us or leave a message and we will get back in 1 to 2 business days.
